History

Art House was founded in 2010, a small workshop having unique product design. We adopted a philosophy to be the leading manufacturer in Egypt with a large showroom network which covers all over Egypt. Therefore and within thirteen years of planning with a strategy and hard dedicated work we became one of the largest B2B, B2C Furniture Company in Egypt.

Growth

As a result of our dedicated work and our clients' trust, ART House was able to increase its investment heavily and expand its capacity, which allowed us to design a unique diversified portfolio, and made ART House the first choices and destination for most celebrities, artists and athlete.
